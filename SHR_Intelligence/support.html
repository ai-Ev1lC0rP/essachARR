Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with SHR Intelligence?

To get started, simply sign up for an account on our website and choose a plan that fits your needs. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process and help you set up your AI helpers.

Can I customize my AI helpers?

Yes, all our AI helpers can be customized to fit your specific needs and workflows. You can adjust their settings, train them on your company's data, and integrate them with your existing tools.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 customer support through email, phone, and live chat. Our team of experts is always ready to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.

Is my data safe with SHR Intelligence?

Absolutely. We take data security very seriously and use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. Our AI helpers are designed with privacy in mind and adhere to strict data protection guidelines.
